Description

Swipr makes cleaning your camera roll fast, fun, and effortless. Just swipe right to keep a photo, or swipe left to delete it, simple as that. Free up storage, remove duplicates, and keep only your best shots with a few quick swipes.

Features

-Swipe to decide: Right to keep, left to delete

-Instant cleanup: Quickly clear blurry, duplicate, or unwanted photos

-Smart grouping: Review your photos in batches for faster decisions

-Storage insights: Track how much space you save

-Super simple design: Clean, fast, and satisfying to use

Stop scrolling through thousands of photos. Start cleaning your gallery the fun way, with Swipr.
