Privacy Policy for Swipr

Last updated: December 3, 2025

Swipr ("the App") is developed by the Swipr Team ("we", "us", or "our").
We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

Swipr does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Photos

  • The App accesses your photos only locally on your device.
  • Your images never leave your device.
  • No photos are uploaded, stored, or shared with us or third parties.

Analytics (If Applicable)

If you enable Apple-provided analytics, anonymous usage data may be shared with us through Apple.
No personally identifiable information is included.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect or store any personal information, we do not use or process any personal data.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share any data with:

  • Advertisers
  • Analytics companies (except optional Apple analytics)
  • Third-party service providers
  • Cloud storage providers

4. Permissions

Swipr requires:

  • Photo Library Access — to display your photos so you can swipe to keep or delete them.

This permission is used solely for the core functionality of the app.

5. Children’s Privacy

Swipr does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
The app contains no tracking and no online features.
